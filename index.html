History

Durham House of Pets was founded in 1995 by Ardalan Mahdavieh and it is the only pet store in Durham. DHP originally only sold dogs and cats and related products. However, since 2008, DHP started selling 23 different kinds of fish and many fish related products.

Why Use Us

Durham House of Pets is the biggest pet store in the state of New Hampshire. DHOP offers a wide range of products so consumers can choose the product that is best for them and their pets. DHOP has won many awards, Including New England’s Best Pet Store in 2009,2016 and New Hampshire’s best customer service in 2012,2015.
